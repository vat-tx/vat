Jeu No. 47

Your mana is showing in spurious parallel to other
concepts of life force, energy, spirits that possess
bodies they move around in “the world.” As we see
further into the past and future, the tree meets earth
or explodes into the sky depending on perspective.
In any case, it is inarguably there, and is extended.
Don’t act like man is not the measure of all things.
That is a revolutionary statement, and a lie as well,
but why should the truth be so important? It’s not
my idea, just one that I often play my games with.

Sam Robinson

Jeu No. 56

Check my ankle and discover
square of opposition, inscribed
by hand, by drunken hand!
I think it came out alright, even
if I didn’t know what it meant.
Now I know and don’t believe,
I’m more of a coincidentia guy.
I saw the moon at midday,
I saw the black Sun! It’s ok.
It doesn’t have to mean so much.
It’s just a pretty symbol on my flesh.
